Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 April 2026

This privacy policy describes how this application Solvle! handles information. Solvle! is a word game helper and is designed to be used without requiring you to provide any personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location).

The App does not require you to create an account or sign in, and it does not access your contacts, photos, files, or other data on your device.

Usage Data and Analytics

The App does not use any third‑party analytics services and does not track your activity across apps or websites.

Advertising

The App does not display third‑party advertisements and does not share any data with advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. However, it has been designed so that it can be used without providing any personal information. Because we do not collect personal data, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Sharing

Since the App does not collect any personal information, no data is shared with third parties.

Security

As the App does not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data to secure on our servers. All gameplay data (such as scores or settings) is stored locally on your device, if at all.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
